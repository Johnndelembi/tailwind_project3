What is Wellness?

Wellness signifies holistic well-being, encompassing an individual's mental, emotional, physical, occupational, intellectual, and spiritual dimensions. Each facet of wellness significantly impacts the overall quality of life, underscoring the importance of comprehensive consideration. In the absence of well-being, mental health disorders prevail, impeding human emotions, cognition, and behavior, thereby diminishing cognitive daily functioning. Consequently, wellness services address the complete spectrum of emotional, mental, physical, spiritual, environmental, occupational, cultural, and religious well-being, empowering individuals to lead purposeful and productive lives.
